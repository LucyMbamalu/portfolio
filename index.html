Excel: Sales & Inventory Management

Business Problem
Product-based businesses often struggle with inaccurate stock records, delayed restocking, expiring items, and limited visibility into sales and profitability.
Solution
Built a comprehensive Excel-based sales and inventory management system that tracks stock-in and stock-out, inventory value, expiry dates, revenue, COGS, and profit in real time. The system includes smart data validation to prevent entry errors, interactive dashboards with slicers for quick analysis, and printable summary reports.
Impact
Improved inventory accuracy, enhanced visibility into product performance, and enabled faster decision-making around restocking, pricing, and expiring inventory.
